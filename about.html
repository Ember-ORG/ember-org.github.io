Program Funcionality

As we mentioned our programs do not leave registry keys on your computer, and

if you are not a tech nerd like we are, you may be wondering what a registry key is!

Registry keys are basically values that are stored on your computer that hold settings for programs and your OS (operating system).

Practically every time you install a program, it should remove all it's traces and registry keys when you run the uninstaller.

Many or most programs however leave these traces on your computer.

Over time, these registry keys build up causing your computer to be slower.

However, our programs will not add registry keys to your computer, therefore keeping your computer clean and speedy.
